Fake Authentication

It performs a fake authentication with aireplay-ng.
There are three kinds of methods for fakeauth: Conservative, standard, and progressive.

Conservative

It executes aireplay-ng --fakeauth 6000 -e Host_SSID -a Host_MAC -h FAKE_MAC WIFI_INTERFACE
This executes fakeauth with a delay of 6000

Progressive

It executes aireplay-ng --fakeauth 0 -e Host_SSID -a Host_MAC -h FAKE_MAC WIFI_INTERFACE
This executes fakeauth with a delay of 0

Standard

It executes aireplay-ng --fakeauth 5 -o 10 -q 1 -e Host_SSID -a Host_MAC -h FAKE_MAC WIFI_INTERFACE
This executes fakeauth with a delay of 5 launching 10 packet for every attempt and waiting 1 second between each packet.
